How It Works

The SOKY Ritual — Five Steps.

Every product. Always this order. The ritual never changes — it's the shared language of portable immersion care.

1
Don
Fold the cuff inward — into itself. Apply lotion if desired, grip the fold, and slide the glove on. Each finger fully into its sheath.
2
Fill
Hold the cuff seal intact. Bring the opening under the tap. Fill to your preferred level and temperature.
3
Seal
Ball your fist — if the glove swells, you're sealed. The cuff responds to pressure by holding more firmly, not less.
4
Soak
15–20 minutes of localized immersion. Walk. Read. Rest. The ritual doesn't ask you to stop — it moves with you.
5
Drain
Over a sink, slide off and pour out. Rinse, air dry, pack away. A clean exit invites return.
Don — The Critical First Step

The cuff must always be folded inward — into itself to form the watertight/airtight seal. This step is non-intuitive. It is where the product is either used correctly or not at all. With the cuff folded, pinch and guide your hand in steadily until the opening rests flush around the wrist — no kinks, no gaps.

Enhancement System

Seven ways into the water.

What goes into the water defines the ritual. Same garment. Same port. Same five-step ritual. Every mode unlocks a different outcome.

Heat
Circulation · Muscle Relief
Warm water (38–42°C) increases local blood flow, relaxes muscle tissue, and supports joint mobility. SOKY's sealed environment retains heat significantly longer than an open basin — a 15-minute warm soak in a SOKY glove maintains therapeutic temperature throughout the session without reheating. Ideal for morning stiffness, post-work hand fatigue, and arthritis support routines.
Cool
Inflammation · Recovery
Cold immersion (10–15°C) reduces acute inflammation, numbs pain signals, and accelerates recovery after exercise or injury. SOKY's insulated seal keeps cold water cold — no melting ice diluting the effect. Contrast bathing (alternating warm and cool soaks) is a clinician-recommended modality that SOKY makes practical for the first time at home. Used by athletes, PT patients, and anyone managing repetitive strain.
Electro
Iontophoresis · TENS · EMS
The Model E clinical tier transforms SOKY into a wearable electrotherapy platform. Iontophoresis — the gold standard for hyperhidrosis — delivers 80%+ sweat reduction via low-level electrical current through mineral-rich water. TENS (pain management) and EMS (muscle activation) modes use the same sealed liquid-electrode environment. The universal port accepts the electrode cap without modifying the garment. Covered by US Patent 11,602,631 B2 through 2043.
Botanicals
Herbal · Skin Nourishment
Chamomile, calendula, lavender, green tea, oat extract — botanical soaks have been used across cultures for centuries. SOKY's reduced water volume means every drop of botanical infusion contacts the skin at higher concentration than a traditional basin. A single herbal tea bag in a SOKY glove delivers more active contact per square centimeter than the same bag in a two-liter bowl. Gentle enough for daily use. Formulated packs coming with SOKY Plus.
Salts
Mineral · Epsom · Dead Sea
Epsom salt (magnesium sulfate) is the most widely used soak additive in the world — and SOKY makes it dramatically more efficient. A tablespoon of Epsom salt in a SOKY glove achieves the same concentration as a full cup in a foot basin. Dead Sea salts, Himalayan pink salt, and mineral blends all work the same way. The sealed environment prevents evaporation and maintains mineral contact throughout the session. Supports muscle recovery, skin softening, and general relaxation.
Actives
OTC & Rx Precision Delivery
Salicylic acid, urea, menthol, camphor, lidocaine analogs, antifungal agents — concentrated active ingredients formulated specifically for the SOKY liquid environment. Because SOKY uses less water than a traditional basin, your actives work harder at higher concentration right where they're needed. Clinical-grade soak packs are in development for users who want pharmacy-level precision in a home routine.
Peptides
Transdermal · Precision Skincare
The most exciting frontier in portable skincare. SOKY's sealed environment increases skin hydration by 300–400%, dramatically improving how well peptides absorb into your skin. This opens the door to collagen-stimulating peptides (Argireline, Matrixyl) for anti-aging, barrier-repair peptides for eczema, growth factor peptides for post-surgical recovery, copper peptides for circulation, and neuropeptide solutions for hyperhidrosis. Dedicated peptide soak packs are coming — designed to pair with your glove for targeted, clinical-grade results at home.
The Volume Advantage — Concentration by Design

Every SOKY garment holds a fraction of the fluid volume required by a conventional foot bath or hand soak basin. This is not a limitation — it is a delivery mechanism. A standard dose formulated for a two-liter basin, used in a SOKY glove containing only a cup of water, achieves a significantly higher active concentration at the treatment site with no increase in ingredient cost.

Every prior wearable immersion device failed when movement created peel forces at the seal. SOKY inverts the problem: internal pressure enters a pocket between cuff layers and pushes the inner layer more firmly against the skin. The more pressure, the tighter the seal.

Frequently Asked Questions

Questions answered.

Everything you need to know before your first soak.

What can SOKY help with?›
SOKY supports personal care routines for hands and feet — including hydration, relaxation, post-exercise recovery, and general skin wellness. It pairs with salts, botanicals, and warm water to create a portable immersion experience.
Is SOKY latex-free?›
Yes. SOKY is made from medical-grade silicone. It is latex-free, BPA-free, and allergy-friendly.
Can I use hot or cold water?›
Yes — SOKY supports both warm and cool soaks. Use water at a temperature comfortable to touch. Avoid boiling water. Cool soaks are great for inflammation and post-exercise recovery.
Is SOKY safe for sensitive skin?›
SOKY is made from skin-safe, medical-grade silicone designed for extended contact. If you have specific skin sensitivities, consult your dermatologist before use.
What happens when a drop sells out?›
SOKY ships in limited drops of 200 units every three months. When a drop sells out, you can pre-order for the next batch at the same price — your order locks in your spot and ships when that batch is ready. Drop 1 ships April 2026, Drop 2 ships July 2026, Drop 3 ships October 2026.
Will SOKY leak?›
When donned correctly — cuff folded inward, fingers fully seated — the patented inversion seal holds firmly. The seal actually tightens with movement. Correct donning is the key step.
Can I use salts or additives?›
Absolutely. Epsom salts, essential oils, botanical extracts, and other soak additives work beautifully. SOKY's lower water volume actually concentrates the active ingredients for more effective delivery.
How long should I soak?›
15–20 minutes is the recommended session length. You can adjust based on your comfort and routine.
Can I walk or use my hands?›
Yes — that's the whole point. SOKY maintains the seal while you move freely. Read, walk, work on your phone, or just rest. The ritual doesn't ask you to stop.
