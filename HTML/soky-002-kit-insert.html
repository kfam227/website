The SOKY Ritual — Five Steps. Always This Order.
01 — Don
Fold the cuff inward — into itself. Pinch the fold, guide each finger fully into its sheath. Cuff rests flush at the wrist. No kinks, no gaps.
02 — Fill
Grip the folded cuff. Bring opening under the tap. Fill to your preferred level and temperature. Add salts, botanicals, or emollients with the water.
03 — Seal
Ball your fist. If the glove swells slightly — you're sealed. The cuff responds to pressure by holding more firmly, not less.
04 — Soak
15–20 minutes of localized immersion. Walk. Read. Rest. The environment holds as you move.
05 — Drain
Over a sink, slide off and pour out. Rinse inside, air dry fully, store unfolded.
Critical — Donning

The cuff must always be folded inward — into itself to form the seal. Never outward. This is where the product works or doesn't.

Peptides
300–400% hydration boost for peptide delivery.
Care & Storage
  • Rinse with clean water after every session
  • Air dry fully before storing — never damp
  • Store unfolded with cuff extended
  • Avoid petroleum-based products or bleach
  • Inspect port and cuff before each session
  • 90+ sessions with proper care
  • Do not use with open wounds without consulting your healthcare provider
Drying & Storage Tip: After draining and rinsing, use the clip hook to hang the glove open for air drying. Once dry, store in the travel bag with mesh panel facing up — or clip the bag to a closet rod to keep it accessible.

⚠ Safety & Warnings
  • ·Slip hazard. Drips may occur — wipe spills immediately on hard flooring.
  • ·Leaks possible. Seal is pressure-responsive, not guaranteed leak-proof. Monitor during initial sessions.
  • ·Temperature. Do not exceed 110°F (43°C). Test with free hand or thermometer. Neuropathy/diabetes: always use thermometer.
  • ·Session limit. Do not exceed 30 min. Prolonged immersion causes skin maceration.
  • ·No electronics. Do not handle plugged-in devices — risk of electric shock.
  • ·Reduced dexterity. Do not drive or operate tools. Remove if numbness or tingling occurs.
  • ·Sharp objects. Do not handle knives or abrasive surfaces — garment is not puncture-proof.
  • ·Children & medical. Not a toy. Patch-test new additives. Consult provider for open wounds or vascular conditions.
