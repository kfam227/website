How the Seal Works

The inversion frustal cuff folds inward, creating a pocket between cuff layers. Internal pressure enters this pocket and pushes the inner layer more firmly against the skin. The more pressure, the tighter the seal. No clamps. No adhesives. No power.

Medical-Grade Silicone

Latex-free. BPA-free. Allergy-friendly. Built for 100+ sessions. One size (M) shipping now — additional sizes Batch 2.

How It Works

Five steps. Every product. Always this order.

The ritual is the platform's shared language. It never changes across any product, user, or context.

1
Don
Fold cuff inward. Slide on. Fingers into sheaths.
2
Fill
Hold seal. Fill under tap to preferred level.
3
Seal
Ball fist — if glove swells, you're sealed.
4
Soak
15–20 min. Walk. Read. Rest. Moves with you.
5
Drain
Slide off, pour out, rinse, air dry.
